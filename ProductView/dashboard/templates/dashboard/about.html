About ProductView

ProductView is a smart web application designed to help users make informed food choices by simply scanning the barcode of any product. With real-time health analysis, it determines whether a product is healthy or not based on its ingredients and nutritional values.

Our goal is to empower individuals to lead healthier lifestyles by offering instant feedback and comparative analysis If none are healthy, we’ll let you know – with reasons.

Whether you're shopping in a store or checking your pantry, ProductView is your go-to tool for transparent food insights, helping you make better dietary decisions.

Start scanning, stay informed, and choose wisely – with ProductView.
